KMCLU Facilities

Hostel:
- Separate hostels for boys and girls
- Boys hostel capacity ~400 students
- Girls hostel capacity ~300 students
- Mess facility available (compulsory)
- Free Wi-Fi, gym, indoor games
- CCTV security available
- Hostel fee approx:
  - New student: ₹14050/year
  - Renewal: ₹13050/year
  - PhD Single: ₹24700/year
  - PhD Double: ₹15100/year

Student Facility Center:
- Central hub for student services
- Supports academic and personal development
- Includes cultural and social spaces

Canteen:
- Hygienic and affordable food available
- Comfortable environment for students

Bank:
- ICICI Bank available inside campus
- Account opening and financial services available

Library / Study Area:
- Study environment available inside facility center
- Helps in research and academic work

Gymnasium:
- Fitness and recreational facility available

Health Centre:
- Medical support for students and staff
- Emergency services available

Art Gallery:
- Exhibition space for student creativity
- Cultural programs and workshops organized

Museum:
- Displays historical artifacts and cultural heritage
- Includes coins, manuscripts, paintings, sculptures
- Supports research and learning

Other Facilities:
- Wi-Fi enabled campus
- Indoor sports
- Cultural activities
- Safe and secure campus
