KMCLU Admission Portal Information

Admission Session:
- Admission for academic session 2026-27 is conducted online.

Application Process:
- Students must register on the official admission portal.
- After registration, login and fill the application form.
- Upload required documents.
- Submit the form before the deadline.

Registration Deadline:
- Last date for registration is 31 May 2026.

Required Documents:
- Scanned Photograph (less than 500 KB)
- Scanned Signature (less than 500 KB)
- Educational Documents (less than 1024 KB)
- Category / Experience Certificates (if applicable)

Admission Portal Features:
- Students can track their admission status online.
- Real-time updates are provided.
- Notifications are available for each step.
